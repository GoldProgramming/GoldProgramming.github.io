Hello, welcome to GoldProgramming!

GoldProgramming is a group of programmers using the Lua language, ported to Computercraft. We also offer some software in other languages.

Learn more!

About Us

GoldProgramming, a group of programmers for ComputerCraft, has now come online to the internet. There are currently three members: Death, Agoldfish, and Csstform. If you would like to join, please go to the Contact Us section.

These members take programs that are requested by others and program them. Some programs include a Luasocket webserver, a todo list for Pocket Computers, and a new BIOS.lua and operating system. Most programs are hosted on the GitHub repositories of the users listed below.
